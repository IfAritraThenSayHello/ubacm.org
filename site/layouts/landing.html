What is UB ACM?

Our mission is to empower student peers and help them find their path in computer science. We do so by running a variety of student-led initiatives and events.

UB ACM is free to join for all University at Buffalo students of any major. Stay updated with UB ACM's in-person events and meet peers online at our Slack workspace!
